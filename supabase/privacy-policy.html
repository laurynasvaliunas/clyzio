4. Third-Party Services & Data Processors

We use the following third-party data processors. Each has been evaluated for GDPR compliance. We do not sell your data to any third party.

Supabase
Database, Authentication & Realtime
Stores all user data, trip records, messages. Database hosted in EU (West). Supabase Auth handles password hashing and JWT tokens.
Anthropic Claude
AI Commute & Carpool Matching
Receives your name, home/work addresses, commuting habits, and vehicle data to generate carpool suggestions and commute plans. Data is used for inference only and not retained by Anthropic for training.
Mapbox
Mapping & Geocoding
Receives origin/destination GPS coordinates to render maps, calculate routes, and reverse-geocode pickup locations to human-readable addresses.
Sentry
Error & Crash Reporting
Receives crash reports, stack traces, and session replay data. Hosted in Germany (EU). Your user ID is sent but no names or emails. Data retained 30 days by default.
Expo
Push Notifications & OTA Updates
Expo's push service routes notifications to Apple APNs and Google FCM using your push token. Expo Updates delivers over-the-air app updates.
Apple APNs / Google FCM
Push Notification Delivery
Notification payload (title, body, screen deep-link) is delivered through Apple's and Google's push infrastructure to your device.

We also share limited profile data (name, vehicle details, phone number) directly with other Clyzio users once a carpool match is confirmed. You control what vehicle and contact information is visible by editing your profile.

8. Security
  • All data is transmitted over TLS 1.2+ encrypted connections.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt via Supabase Auth — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • Database access is controlled by Row Level Security (RLS) — users can only read and write their own data.
  • Location data is only accessible to other users when a carpool match is confirmed.
  • Vehicle details (licence plate, colour) are only shown to your confirmed carpool partners.
  • AI inputs sent to Anthropic are transmitted securely and not used for model training.
  • Crash data sent to Sentry contains only your opaque user ID, not your name or email.

9. Children's Privacy

Clyzio is designed for workplace commute coordination and is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@clyzio.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your primary data is stored in EU data centres (Supabase EU West region; Sentry Germany). Some data is processed outside the EEA:

  • Anthropic (USA) — AI inference. Anthropic is certified under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and uses Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international transfers.
  • Mapbox (USA) — Geocoding and routing. Mapbox complies with GDPR via SCCs.
  • Expo (USA) — Push notification routing. Expo uses SCCs for transfers to the EEA.

All international transfers are governed by appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR Chapter V.
